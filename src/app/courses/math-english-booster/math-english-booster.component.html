Maths & English booster

At Value Learning the extra tuition provided is designed to complement the school curriculum throughout the year. However, developing confidence, knowledge and study skills is a long- term process. The longer a pupil has to prepare and really get to master their subject, the more confidence and understanding they will have developed.
Value Learning maths and english Booster Classes cater for year 3 to year 6 and our learning materials are linked to the national curriculum.
Our experienced maths tutors are enthusiastic about their subject and craft the tuition to ensure your child understands the difficult concepts in some maths topics and build confidence sharing their passion and instilling the “I can do” attitude.
Value Learning Tuition breeds success and all our pupils soon get noticed in school for their rapid progression in school.
In Value Learning booster classes, we cover Key Stage 2 National Curriculum in Maths and English. Our main emphasis are on the following.

English
  • Reading
  • Comprehension
  • Grammar and spelling
  • Punctuation
At Value Learning, we also ensure your child’s literacy skills are developed through our in-house materials which complement the National Curriculum.
Maths
  • Number
  • Measures
  • Geometry
  • Statistics
  • Word problems

The session lasts for one hour and ten minutes a week and each lesson will be targeted towards both subjects. Homework is set after each lesson and would usually take between 20 and 30 minutes a day to complete.

Our classes are kept small with up to 12 pupils and a maximum ratio of one tutor to 8 pupils, so we are able to meet the individual needs of each child.
