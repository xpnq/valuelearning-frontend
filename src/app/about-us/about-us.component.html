Director of Learning

Temi Joseph

MSc PGCE.

Temi has extensive teaching experience that spans over 20 years. As one of the Value Learning Senior tutors, she has helped many students with varying abilities to achieve their educational goals as well as prepare pupils for the 11 plus entrance exams. She is passionate about stimulating pupils to learn and seeing students realise their full potential.

Temi is a mentor to many young people and has numerous tuition success stories in helping pupils develop confidence and overcome challenges. She has a unique way of drawing out the best out of every child and encourages them to aim higher. Her enthusiasm and bespoke methods of teaching has proven to be a “hit” amongst her students.
