11+ Intensive Summer Course Information 

5th – 9th August 2019.
Monday – Friday 9.30am – 12.30 pm.

11+ Verbal Reasoning

Emphasis will be on all aspects including the following:
  • Synonyms and antonyms
  • Odd one out
  • Word association
  • Jumbled up sentences
  • Double meaning
  • Spelling etc

11+ Non- verbal Reasoning

Children will:
  • Learn how to do all questions types including rotation of 3D shapes and nets.
  • Engage in exercises and activities to help with sequencing and visual perception.
  • Learn how to use the correct strategies in order to improve accuracy.

11+ Maths/Numerical reasoning

Revision topics include:
  • Number & Algebra
  • Shape & Space
  • Measures & Handling data.
  • Logic & Problem solving
Children will learn useful quick methods and strategies in order to tackle word problems with confidence.

11+ English

We will cover the major aspects of the 11+ English exams:
  • Comprehension
  • Cloze tests & spelling
  • Grammar & word building puzzles.

Timing and Exam Technique

Children will be given help with time management in order to facilitate the completion of the 11+ tests as much as possible, within the time allowed. Your child will enjoy the games and activities during the week to keep them engaged and make learning fun. There will be homework ( practice papers and timed exercises) given everyday which could last up to 1.5 hours.
